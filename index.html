DICES

RULES

This game is really simple, after clicking "Play" button dices will be thrown for you and your enemy after 3 rounds whoever have higher summary of throws win. If you win you will get win points for which you can upgrade your dice for better chance of throwing high numbers. Both you and your Enemy have 10 lives if you lose them all you die but if you beat your enemy 10 times before that you win and survives.
